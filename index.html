Morpheus TV



Android app to keep track of your movies and tvshows using Trakt.tv.
Optimized for TV and DPAD remote control navigation, but it also works great on touch devices.

The author does not host or upload any of the content returned by this service.
Everything is retrieved from third-party websites like Trakt, TMDB and others.
